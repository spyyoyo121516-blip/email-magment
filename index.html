How to Create an Email Account

Step-by-step guides for the most popular email providers

Most Popular
G
Gmail
Google Account
  1. Click the button below to open Gmail signup
  2. Enter your First Name and Last Name
  3. Pick a username — this will be your @gmail.com address
  4. Create a strong password and confirm it
  5. For phone number — click "Skip" if available, or use a recovery email instead
  6. Accept Terms of Service and you are done!
Open Gmail Signup →
Popular
O
Outlook
Microsoft Account
  1. Click the button below to open Outlook signup
  2. Click "Get a new email address"
  3. Choose your @outlook.com address
  4. Create a strong password
  5. Enter your name and date of birth
  6. Solve the puzzle/captcha — no phone needed!
Open Outlook Signup →
Y
Yahoo Mail
Yahoo Account
  1. Click the button below to open Yahoo signup
  2. Fill in your name, email address, password, and birthday
  3. Phone number may be required — use any number to get a code
  4. Verify the code and you are in!
Open Yahoo Signup →
No Phone Needed
P
ProtonMail
Encrypted & Private
  1. Click the button below to open ProtonMail signup
  2. Choose the FREE plan
  3. Pick your @proton.me username
  4. Create a password — no phone or other email needed!
  5. Solve a simple puzzle (not a phone captcha)
  6. Done! Fully private email, zero phone verification
Open ProtonMail Signup →
No Phone Needed
T
Tuta (Tutanota)
Secure Email
  1. Click the button below to open Tuta signup
  2. Choose the FREE plan
  3. Pick your @tuta.com username
  4. Create a password — NO phone, NO other email needed
  5. Accept terms and your account is created
  6. Wait 48 hours for account activation (anti-spam measure)
Open Tuta Signup →
Pro Trick
∞
Gmail Unlimited Aliases
One Gmail = Infinite Emails
  1. If you already have one Gmail like john@gmail.com
  2. You can use john+shop@gmail.com for shopping sites
  3. Or john+social@gmail.com for social media
  4. ALL emails go to your same inbox — john@gmail.com
  5. Each alias works as a separate email for signups!
  6. No limit — create as many as you want, instantly
Open Gmail →

💡 Pro Tips

ProtonMail and Tuta are the easiest — no phone number, no other email required.
For Gmail, try signing up from a fresh browser or incognito mode — sometimes the phone step can be skipped.
Use the Gmail Alias trick to get unlimited email addresses from just one account!
